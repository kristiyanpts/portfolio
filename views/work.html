JavaScript Chat Application
This project was made for the "Second Natination Conference 'Information Technology and Automation' 2023. It won an award for the excellent work (1 of only 15 awards in the conference). It was made all by myself under the mentorship of my teacher Nadezhda Manchevska. Its main goal is to showcase what a person can do with just two months of studying web development. It has all the features your everyday chat has, user profile, user customization, your very own chats, etc. Of cours it has its downsides but its still a work in progress! You can had to the github repo to check it out for yourself.
